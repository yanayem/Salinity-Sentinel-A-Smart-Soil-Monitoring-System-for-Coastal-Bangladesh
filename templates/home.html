Salinity Sentinel – A Smart Soil Monitoring System for Coastal Bangladesh

The coastal regions of Bangladesh are severely affected by soil salinity, which directly hampers agricultural productivity and the livelihoods of farmers.

Salinity Sentinel is a smart, low-cost soil monitoring system that uses sensors and Python-based software to measure soil salinity, moisture, temperature, and pH in real-time — helping farmers take preventive action and protect their crops.
